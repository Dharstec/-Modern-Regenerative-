The O Shot®, also known as the Orgasm Shot, is a groundbreaking non-surgical vaginal rejuvenation treatment. This innovative procedure utilizes the healing potential of your own blood by transforming it into Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP). The PRP, rich in essential nutrients and peptides, is then skillfully injected into the vaginal and clitoral tissue. The result is not only a rejuvenated intimate area but also a remarkable enhancement in sexual well-being. Patients who undergo the O Shot® commonly report increased libido, alongside the added benefit of intensified orgasms.

The Treatment

Assessment and Preparation

Once you're approved as an O Shot® candidate, a topical numbing agent is applied externally to ensure comfort during the procedure.

Blood Collection and Processing

A small blood sample is drawn and processed in a centrifuge to isolate the gold-colored, nutrient-dense plasma, which will be used for the treatment.

PRP Injection

The PRP serum is skillfully injected into the clitoris and vaginal opening to activate stem cells and kickstart tissue regeneration.
