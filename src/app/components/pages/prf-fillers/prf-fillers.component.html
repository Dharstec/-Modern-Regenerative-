PRF Fillers, short for Platelet-Rich Fibrin Fillers, represent a cutting-edge approach to facial rejuvenation. Derived directly from your own blood, PRF Fillers specialize in reviving areas that bear the marks of time. In contrast to Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP), PRF offers a more concentrated blend of white blood cells, stem cells, fibrin, and growth factors – all vital components for stimulating collagen and revitalizing the skin. This makes PRF an all-natural filler that harnesses your body's healing mechanisms for authentic, age-defying results.

The Treatment

Blood Sample Extraction

In the initial step, a small blood sample is taken from the patient, usually from the arm. This sample serves as the source of essential components needed for the PRF treatment.

Centrifugation

The extracted blood sample undergoes precise centrifugation, a process that separates the blood into its various components. This separation isolates the growth factors, fibrins, and platelets, which are critical for the rejuvenation process.

Expert Injection

Skilled medical professionals with expertise in PRF treatments administer the PRF solution. They use precise injection techniques to ensure the solution reaches the target areas effectively, such as lips, cheeks, jawline, temple, under eyes, nasolabial and chin.

Gradual Restoration

Unlike synthetic fillers that provide immediate results, PRF operates at a cellular level. It stimulates collagen production and gradually restores volume and youthful definition over a period of time, leading to natural-looking and the results lasts 6-12 weeks.

Natural Collagen Stimulation

PRF stimulates your body's natural collagen production, revitalizing and reshaping facial areas.

Safety Profile

With PRF, you're using your own blood, minimizing the risk of allergic reactions.

Non-Surgical Approach

PRF Fillers offer a non-surgical and minimally invasive approach to facial rejuvenation.

Adaptable Solutions

PRF treatments can be tailored to address individual concerns.

Lasting Results

Although gradual, PRF's outcomes persist due to sustained collagen activation.

Minimal Side Effects

PRF's organic nature ensures fewer complications.

Is It Right For You?

PRF Fillers are ideal for individuals looking to regain or enhance facial volume and definition naturally, without synthetic fillers. However, it might not be suitable for

  • Those with active cancer or undergoing chemotherapy.
  • Current smokers
  • Those with unrealistic expectations.
  • Those with Hemophiliacs
  • People with compromised immune systems
  • Individuals on high doses of blood thinners
