The Treatment

Blood Sample Collection

The process commences with the collection of a minor blood sample, typically from the arm. This sample serves as the source of essential components required for the PRP treatment.

Precise Centrifugation

The extracted blood sample undergoes meticulous centrifugation, a process that separates it into its vital components. This separation isolates the growth factors, stem cells, and platelets, which are crucial for the rejuvenation process.

Expert Injection

Skilled medical professionals, well-versed in PRP treatments, administer the PRP solution using precise injection techniques to ensure the solution effectively reaches the targeted areas, such as the neck.

Progressive Restoration

In contrast to immediate synthetic fillers that offer instant results, PRP operates at a cellular level. It stimulates collagen production and gradually restores volume and youthful definition over time.

Natural Regeneration

PRP stimulates the body's own collagen production, subtly restoring volume and firmness to the neck.

Safety First

As PRP is derived from your own body, the risk of allergic reactions or other adverse events is significantly reduced.
