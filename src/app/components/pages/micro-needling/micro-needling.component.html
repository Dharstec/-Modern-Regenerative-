Our microneedling procedure is performed by trained providers using Dr. Pen, a revolutionary skincare microneedling device that stimulates collagen production with tiny sterilized needles. This technique aims to tighten, lift, and rejuvenate the skin.

The Treatment

Micro-Injuries for Collagen Stimulation

Microneedling introduces controlled micro-injuries via tiny needles to stimulate collagen production, a process known as collagen induction therapy. This enhances natural collagen, diminishes scars, reduces fine lines and wrinkles, and balances skin tone.

Elevate the Microneedling Experience with PRP

The combination of the Dr. Pen microneedling device and PRP serum accelerates skin rejuvenation. The Dr. Pen creates micro-wounds, driving collagen synthesis and augmenting the skin's youthful appearance.

Microneedling Beyond the Face

While most commonly used on the face, microneedling can also be beneficial for the neck and chest, promoting a more youthful appearance in these areas

Harnessing the Power of PRP

To maximize outcomes, Dr. Pen is integrated with Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP), leveraging the body's growth factors for skin rejuvenation.
