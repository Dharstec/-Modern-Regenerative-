The Treatment

The PRP Facial is rooted in the principles of regenerative aesthetics and involves a simple process

Blood Extraction

The process begins by drawing a small blood sample from your arm, which serves as the source of vital components required for the PRP Facial

Centrifugation

The enriched plasma is skillfully reintroduced into specific facial areas, ensuring accurate targeting and optimized results

Collagen Stimulation

The blood is then spun down in a centrifuge at high speed to separate the red blood cells from the platelets and plasma, leaving behind platelet-rich plasma filled with growth factors and cytokines.

Plasma Enrichment

This sample is then meticulously refined to concentrate platelets, which are packed with essential growth factors and proteins needed for effective skin rejuvenation

Precise Reintroduction

The enriched plasma is skillfully reintroduced into specific facial areas, ensuring accurate targeting and optimized results
