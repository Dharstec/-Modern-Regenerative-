The Treatment

Microthermal Zones

Tixel treatment creates microthermal zones on the skin's surface using a specialized titanium tip. These zones are essential for stimulating natural healing processes.

Collagen Stimulation

By applying thermal mechanical energy, Tixel encourages the production of collagen. This crucial protein helps the skin become smoother, firmer, and more youthful in appearance.

Non-Invasive Approach:

Tixel is non-invasive, providing a pain-free and comfortable treatment experience for patients. It doesn't require needles, incisions, or invasive procedures.

Safety and Minimal Downtime

Tixel distinguishes itself from traditional CO2 lasers by offering safe treatment without causing skin charring or post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. While it may have slightly longer downtime (72 hours), the results are highly comparable.

Scar Reduction

Tixel is highly effective in reducing the appearance of scars, including acne scars and stretch marks, providing improved skin texture and tone.

Safety and Comfort

Patients can enjoy a safe and pain-free treatment experience with Tixel, thanks to its non-invasive approach and minimal risk of complications.

Quick Procedure

Tixel treatments are relatively quick, typically lasting between 30 to 60 minutes, making it convenient to schedule and incorporate into your daily routine.

Reduced Downtime

While there is some downtime post-treatment (72 hours), the minimal interruption to daily activities makes it a practical option for many patients.

Improvement in Skin Appearance

Tixel treatment stimulates collagen production, leading to smoother, firmer, and younger-looking skin, with a visible decrease in the appearance of acne scars and fine lines.

No Need for Laser Safety Measures

Unlike traditional lasers, Tixel doesn't emit lasers, eliminating the need for laser safety measures. This contributes to a more comfortable and anxiety-free experience during treatment.
